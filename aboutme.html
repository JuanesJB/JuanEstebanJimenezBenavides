About me

In addition to my interest in technology, I have two other passions in my life: cooking and tennis. I love experimenting in the kitchen, mixing ingredients and creating delicious dishes. I especially enjoy preparing meals for my friends and family, as I love to see how they enjoy my culinary creations.

Tennis is another important aspect in my life. Playing this sport gives me great satisfaction and allows me to stay physically active. Participating in matches and tournaments helps me to improve my skills and also to maintain a balance between study and sport.

In addition to my personal interests, I also have an important role as representative of my department for the 2023 period. This position allows me to work closely with my fellow students, represent their interests, and contribute to improving the student experience at our university.

In summary, I am a computer and systems engineering student, a lover of cooking and tennis, and committed to my role as representative of my department. I am excited for the opportunities that come my way and ready to face new challenges in my academic and personal life.
